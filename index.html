Our History

In 2013, we opened our first store in Ypsilanti in a cosy little street near the Grand Market. People from all over the world fell in love with the soft cakes, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apple pies ... The store became a success in no time.

In 2015, no less than 100 pies were sold every day and Bethay's Pie Shop moved into a larger building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own online store, making it possible to Bethany's delicious pies!
